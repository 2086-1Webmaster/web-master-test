Township Tech 2022 Annual Awards Ceremony

Welcome to the 2022 Township Tech Annual Award Ceremony for excellence in providing quality service to our customers and patrons. On this fine evening, we congratulate our employees who have shown superb customer service and efficiency in their work fields and have shown their expertise to the company.

Meet the Contestants

Quadale Dingle

Quandale Dingle hailed from New Orleans and decided to join Township Tech when he graduated from college. Being a graduate LochnLaod University meant that he was very much qualified for the work at Township and qualified he was. He helped boost his team to be recognized throughout all of Township Tech. Now he is nominated to be picked employee of the year.

Gavin

I don't know how Gavin got in here. He doesn't really do much. I guess he works hard. That's good.

Lord Stuttgart Ludwig

Hailing from the faroff lands of Germany, Lord Ludwig joined Township Tech as a customer. He used our services to conduct online meetings with his patients. Now he joined the team and has been working with Township Tech for 20 years. He is the head of his team and manages some of the most important tasks at Township Tech.

Meet the Judges

Leonard Faux Paux

Leonard Faux Paux of the Grand Court is an esteemed member of Township Tech and has been working with us for 200 years. His wisdom and experience make him more than qualified to judge in this year's competitions.

Mighty Edwards

Might Edwards came from the mountains of Edward Rock, the place he was named after. He grew up in a tough area and had to rely on Township Tech to conduct his doctors visits. He was so inspired by Township Tech that he joined the team. Now, 600 years later, he is a judge for the 2022 Township Tech Awards Ceremony.
